GLETSCHER BREWERY

History:

In 1896 José Fischer, a German master brewer, arrived in Chilean Patagonia with the aim of installing the first brewery in the country. With this dream in mind, José founded the southernmost brewery in the world. The brewery is located in the same place since the year it was founded, however, the city grew and caused it to be located in the middle of it, turning the brewery into a symbol for the city and a place with a great history until today. To date we have the production of 3 brands of beer in all its types and with a recognized history.
